News & Events

[10-Oct-2011] Inter-University Enterprise Mobility Software Development Competition 2011 organized by Sri Lanka Association of Software and Services Companies (SLASSCOM) and Motorola Solutions, was won by a team of 4 students ( P.M.U.C.B. Senanayake, E.A.P.S. Rahal, R.B.M.R. Rajapaksha and H.H.T.S. Herath ) from our department. The winning project was titled Greenhouse and Agricultural Management System.

[27-Apr-2011] A team of students from the department has won the Sri Lankan finals of the Microsoft Imagine Cup Software Design Competition, 2011. Now they will move to the world final to be held in New York, in July this year. The team members, all from E/07 batch, are Lahiru Senadheera, Madushanka Upendra, Lakshitha Herath and Dinushan Madhushanka.

[18-Dec-2010] One of the four technical papers presented from our department at the Fifth International Conference on Information and Automation for Sustainability (ICIAfS2010) won the best student paper award for the ICT & Social Transformation Track. This paper, titled Converting Printed Sinhala Documents to Formatted Editable Text, was co-authored by Shahina Ajward, Nalani Jayasundara, Sarasi Madushika and Roshan Ragel.

[15-Nov-2010] The outcome of four student research projects conducted at the department are accepted for publication at the 5th IEEE International Conference on Information and Automation for Sustainability (ICIAfS'10) to be held in Colombo in December 2010.
