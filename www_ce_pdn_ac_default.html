Welcome!

Welcome to the Department of Computer Engineering at the University of Peradeniya. CS (as the department is usually known for historical reasons) is renowned for the quality and intensity of its educational experience.

At present, CS is mostly involved in teaching undergraduate courses for Computer Engineering students. We believe that the information you find here are useful to you.
