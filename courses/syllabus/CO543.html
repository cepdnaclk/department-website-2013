CO543:Image Processing

Course Number : CO543
Course Title :Image Processing
Credits : 3
Prerequisites :EE 385 Digital Signals and Systems OR EE303
No Course Content Time Allocated (hours)
      L&T P&A
01 Introduction

Digital images, Origins and applications of digital image processing, Steps in image processing, Components of an image processing system 

 3

 
02 Digital Image Fundamentals

Visual perception:Structure and characteristics of the human eye; Light and the electromagnetic spectrum, Image sensing and acquisition,  Image sampling and quantization, Basic pixel relationships,  Linear and non-linear operations.

6

 
03

Image enhancement in the Spatial domain

 Basic gray level transformations, Histogram processing, Arithmetic and Logic operations, Spatial filtering: Basics, Smoothing spatial filters, Sharpening spatial filters; Combining spatial enhancement methods.

 6  
04

Image enhancement in the Frequency Domain

2D DFT and its inverse, Filtering, Correspondence between filtering in spatial and frequency domains;  Smoothing frequency domain filters, Sharpening frequency domain filters, Homomorphic filtering, Implementation: Need for padding, Properties of the 2D Fourier Transform, 2D FFT 

6  
05

Color Image Processing

Color Fundamentals, RGB and CMY/CMYK color models, Pseudo-color Image Processing, Basics of full Color Image Processing, Color transformations, Smoothing and Sharpening

 4  
06

Image Compression

Fundamentals, Image compression models, Information theory, error-free compression techniques, lossy compression techniques, compression standards

5  
07
