Configuring mail clients for CE mail
by Ziyan Maraikar - Thursday, 4 April 2013, 10:38 pm
 
If you would like to use a mail client like Thunderbird, Outlook or read mail on you smartphone these are the settings you need to configure:

Incoming server:
Type: IMAP
address: imap.ce.pdn.ac.lk
Port: 143
TLS: enabled

Outgoing server:
Type: SMTP with plaintext authentication
address: smtp.ce.pdn.ac.lk
Port: 587
TLS: enabled
 
Problems with E/10 email accounts
by Admin User - Wednesday, 27 March 2013, 1:10 am
 

If you are unable to send mail from your e10 mail account, check whether your address is shown as "e10xxx@dovecot.local". If so please log in to Roundcube and go to "Settings"-->"Identities". Your email should be changed to "e10xxx@ce.pdn.ac.lk".
